About

When you want your pet to look their best, visit one of our convienient locations in Ypsilanti, or Belleville, Michigan for a bath or groom. We accept cats, dogs an other critters of all shapes and sizes. We also offer walk-in nail trims or filing.

Canine clients throughout Washtenaw and Wayne counties have been enjoying their Dog Grooming experience here for many years, receiving a full range of dog grooming services, including medicated, and fur-whitening washes, as well as ear cleaning, nail clipping, de-shedding, and skin and coat treatments.
